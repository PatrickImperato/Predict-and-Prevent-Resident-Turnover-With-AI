# User Personas

This product focuses on operational roles within property management teams.

The goal is to improve resident retention by giving teams a clearer workflow for identifying risk and acting early.

---

# Persona 1  
Property Manager

## Responsibilities

Property managers are responsible for the daily operations of a property.

Their responsibilities include:

- handling resident issues
- coordinating maintenance
- responding to complaints
- managing lease renewals
- maintaining resident satisfaction

## Pain Points

Property managers face several challenges related to resident retention.

These include:

- issues appear across multiple systems
- complaints are tracked informally
- difficult to see which residents are at risk
- little time to investigate resident history

As a result, many retention opportunities are missed.

## Goals

Property managers want to:

- quickly identify unhappy residents
- resolve problems before renewal time
- maintain strong resident relationships
- reduce turnover at their property

---

# Persona 2  
Operations Manager

## Responsibilities

Operations managers oversee multiple properties and teams.

They focus on operational consistency and performance.

Responsibilities include:

- monitoring property performance
- managing regional staff
- ensuring service standards
- identifying operational risks

## Pain Points

Operations managers often lack visibility into what is happening at the property level.

Common issues include:

- inconsistent resident service workflows
- limited data on intervention outcomes
- difficulty identifying systemic issues across properties

## Goals

Operations managers want to:

- ensure teams follow consistent processes
- detect operational problems early
- reduce avoidable resident turnover
- monitor retention activity across properties

---

# Persona 3  
Asset Manager / Owner

## Responsibilities

Asset managers focus on financial performance and long term property value.

Responsibilities include:

- tracking occupancy and revenue
- monitoring operational performance
- reviewing property risk indicators

## Pain Points

Owners and asset managers often lack visibility into the operational factors that drive turnover.

They typically see results only after turnover occurs.

## Goals

Asset managers want to:

- reduce vacancy loss
- maintain stable occupancy
- understand drivers of turnover
- evaluate whether retention interventions are working
