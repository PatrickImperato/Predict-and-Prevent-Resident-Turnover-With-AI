# Product Requirements Document

Product Name  
HappyCo Resident Retention Platform

---

# Product Overview

The HappyCo Resident Retention Platform is designed to help property teams identify residents at risk of leaving and coordinate retention interventions before turnover occurs.

The platform centralizes resident signals, operational workflows, and service recovery tools into a single system.

The goal is to reduce preventable resident turnover and improve resident satisfaction.

---

# Product Goals

The first version of the product focuses on three goals.

1. Help property teams identify residents who may be at risk of leaving.

2. Provide a structured workflow for responding to retention risks.

3. Improve visibility for managers into resident issues and intervention outcomes.

---

# Success Metrics

The product would be considered successful if it improves key operational metrics.

Examples include:

• reduction in preventable resident turnover  
• increased lease renewal rates  
• faster resolution of resident issues  
• improved resident satisfaction  

Operational metrics may include:

• number of interventions triggered  
• time to intervention after issue detection  
• intervention completion rates  

---

# Target Users

Primary users include:

Property Manager  
Responsible for identifying resident issues and triggering interventions.

Operations Manager  
Responsible for monitoring team activity and ensuring follow through.

Asset Manager  
Responsible for tracking occupancy, retention, and operational risk.

---

# Core Features

The first version of the product includes the following capabilities.

Resident Risk Visibility

A dashboard that highlights residents who may require attention based on signals such as service issues or complaints.

Resident Profile View

A centralized page showing the resident's service history, complaints, and recent interactions.

Intervention Workflow

A structured process for initiating actions such as service recovery, concierge support, or retention incentives.

Service Booking

A workflow that allows residents to schedule support services or follow up actions.

Manager Dashboard

A view that allows managers to monitor intervention activity and ensure follow through.

---

# User Workflow

The expected workflow is:

1. System identifies a resident showing potential dissatisfaction signals.

2. Property manager reviews the resident profile.

3. Manager initiates an intervention.

4. Service or support is delivered.

5. Manager tracks resolution and outcome.

---

# Scope for Version 1

The initial version focuses on enabling a basic retention workflow.

Included:

• resident risk signals  
• intervention workflow  
• service booking  
• manager visibility  

Not included:

• advanced churn prediction models  
• full property management system integration  
• automated intervention recommendations  

---

# Future Opportunities

Future versions of the platform could include:

• machine learning churn prediction  
• automated intervention suggestions  
• deeper analytics on intervention success  
• integration with property management systems
