# Product Roadmap

This roadmap outlines how the HappyCo Resident Retention Platform could evolve over time.

The goal of the roadmap is to show how the product could move from an initial operational workflow into a more advanced retention intelligence system.

---

# Version 1  
Operational Retention Workflow

Focus

Provide property teams with a structured workflow for identifying and responding to resident dissatisfaction.

Key capabilities

• resident issue visibility  
• resident profile view  
• intervention workflow  
• service booking  
• manager dashboard  

Goal

Enable property teams to act earlier when residents show signs of dissatisfaction.

---

# Version 2  
Retention Insights

Focus

Improve visibility into resident trends and intervention outcomes.

Key capabilities

• intervention analytics  
• property level retention dashboards  
• historical intervention tracking  
• performance reporting for managers  

Goal

Help operations teams understand which interventions improve retention.

---

# Version 3  
Predictive Risk Detection

Focus

Introduce predictive signals that identify residents at risk of leaving before issues escalate.

Key capabilities

• churn risk scoring  
• automated risk alerts  
• early intervention recommendations  

Goal

Shift the platform from reactive workflows to proactive retention management.

---

# Version 4  
Automated Retention Operations

Focus

Reduce manual work and scale retention programs across multiple properties.

Key capabilities

• automated intervention triggers  
• AI suggested service recovery actions  
• integration with property management systems  

Goal

Enable property teams to manage retention programs across large portfolios.

---

# Long Term Vision

The long term vision of the platform is to become the operational layer that connects resident experience signals with proactive retention workflows.

The platform would help property teams:

• detect dissatisfaction early  
• coordinate structured interventions  
• measure the effectiveness of retention programs  
• reduce preventable resident turnover
